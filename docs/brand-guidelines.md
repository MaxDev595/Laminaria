# Laminaria brand guidelines v1.0

| Element | Direction |

| Deep background | Abyss `#061A24` |

## Positioning

Laminaria is the webinar platform for distributed teams and independent experts who want a room that
feels composed, inclusive, and under control. It combines dependable live infrastructure with
thoughtful moderation and a calm interface. It never claims that complex live work is magically
effortless; it makes the system state understandable and gives the host precise control.

## Voice

| Trait | We are | We are not |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Calm | Clear about what is happening and what to do next | Cold or vague |
| Capable | Precise, operational, honest about limits | Boastful or overly technical |
| Human | Respectful of attention, timezone, language, and consent | Cute, childish, or chatty |
| Global | Plain international English and natural Russian | US-only idiom or literal translation |

Success is brief: “Your webinar is scheduled.” Errors name the problem and recovery: “We could not
reach LiveKit. Your webinar settings are safe — try connecting again.” AI always labels itself and
states uncertainty.

## Visual identity

The mark is an abstract lamina: three calm vertical fronds cut by a waterline. It must remain a simple
one-color or approved two-color SVG with clear space equal to the mark height. Full wordmark minimum
width is 120px; icon minimum is 24px. Do not add fish, bubbles, mascots, literal seaweed photography,
neon glows, heavy blur, or decorative motion inside the active stage.

Depth comes from low-contrast layers, restrained specular highlights, gentle organic radii, and
motion tied to state. Kelp green indicates action/connection; coral is reserved for important or
destructive moments; status never relies on color alone.

## Motion language

- Direct manipulation: 150–200ms, spring-like but without overshoot.
- Panels and route content: 220–360ms with opacity plus 8–16px movement.
- Ambient light: 10–18 seconds, transform/opacity only, paused offscreen.
- Layout transitions preserve spatial continuity; skeletons avoid cumulative layout shift.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` removes nonessential transforms and ambient movement.

## Accessibility

Minimum contrast is WCAG AA, focus is always visible, touch targets are at least 44px, animations do
not encode the only state signal, controls have accessible names, and all copy exists in EN and RU.
